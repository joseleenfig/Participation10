My Font

For the font I created using the website metaflop, I created a simple bold font. I am using something that looks italic and bold at the same time, for style and also to stand out.
I like the look of the font tilting a bit to show its difference in the rest of the fonts. I used the optical corrections to situate the corners, overshoot and taper. I also increase the contrast, slanting, and superness
for the shape of the text. The proportion of my text was also increases, I used the cap height and bar height. Although I am having trouble linking my new font, that is mostly what I liked about the simple new font I made.
